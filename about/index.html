Our Story

Built on Craftsmanship, Driven by Community

Dutch Painting Services was founded in Yarmouth, Nova Scotia, with a simple mission: to deliver honest, high-quality painting services to our local community. Built on decades of hands-on experience in the trade, we've quickly grown into one of the South Shore's most trusted painting contractors.

Our team brings over 30 years of combined experience to every project. From commercial buildings and retail spaces to family homes and cherished furniture, we bring the same level of care and precision to every job.

We believe in supporting the community that supports us. That's why we source our painting supplies locally whenever possible, keeping business within our region and ensuring we use products we trust.

Today, Dutch Painting Services is proud to serve businesses, families, and individuals from Yarmouth all the way up the southern shore to Halifax — and we're just getting started.
