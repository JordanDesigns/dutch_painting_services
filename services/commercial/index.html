Scope of Work

What We Cover

Our commercial painting services address every aspect of your property's interior and exterior surfaces.

Interior Wall & Ceiling Painting

Complete interior repaints for offices, lobbies, hallways, break rooms, and common areas with minimal disruption to your operations.

Exterior Building Painting

Weather-resistant exterior coatings for commercial buildings, storefronts, and signage. Products chosen for Nova Scotia's coastal climate.

Retail & Storefront Refreshing

Quick-turnaround repaints designed to refresh your retail space and attract customers. We work after hours or on weekends to avoid disruption.

Warehouse & Industrial Coatings

Heavy-duty coatings for warehouses, production floors, and storage facilities. Durable products that withstand high traffic and daily wear.

Spray Painting

Professional spray application for large surfaces that demand even coverage and a smooth, factory-quality finish. Ideal for commercial exteriors.

Tenant & Property Prep

Move-in and move-out painting for commercial landlords and property managers. Fast turnaround to minimize vacancy between tenants.

How We Work

Our Commercial Painting Process

A proven approach that delivers consistent results while respecting your business operations.

01

Initial Consultation & Site Assessment

We visit your property to assess the scope of work, discuss your goals, and understand your schedule and operational needs. We'll take measurements, evaluate surface conditions, and note any special requirements.

02

Detailed Quote & Planning

You'll receive a transparent, itemized quote with no hidden costs. We'll work with you to develop a project timeline that minimizes disruption — including evenings and weekends if needed.

03

Surface Preparation

Proper prep is the foundation of a lasting finish. We clean, sand, patch, prime, and mask every surface thoroughly before any paint is applied. Furniture and fixtures are carefully protected.

04

Professional Application

Using the best method for each surface — brush, roller, or spray — our technicians apply premium coatings with precision. Multiple coats are applied as needed for full, even coverage.

05

Final Inspection & Cleanup

We conduct a thorough walkthrough with you to ensure every detail meets your expectations. The site is left clean and ready for business — we take care of everything.
