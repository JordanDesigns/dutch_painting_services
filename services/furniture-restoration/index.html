Furniture Restoration

Give Your Furniture a Brand New Look

That worn-out dresser, the dated kitchen cabinets, the family heirloom that's seen better days — don't throw them away. At Dutch Painting Services, we specialize in bringing furniture back to life through expert repainting and refinishing.

Working from our dedicated 1,000 square foot facility in Yarmouth, we have the space and equipment to handle pieces of all sizes — from small accent tables to full sets of kitchen cabinetry. Our professional spray booth delivers a factory-smooth finish that brush and roller simply can't match.

Whether you want to modernize a piece with a fresh colour, restore an antique to its former glory, or completely reinvent a room by refinishing your built-ins, we have the skill and experience to make it happen.

How We Work

Our Restoration Process

Every piece goes through a thorough, multi-step process to ensure a beautiful, lasting finish.

01

Consultation & Assessment

Bring your piece to our facility or send us photos to discuss. We'll assess the condition, talk through your vision for the piece, and recommend the best approach — whether it's a full repaint, refinishing, or a more nuanced restoration.

02

Disassembly & Preparation

We carefully disassemble removable components — doors, drawers, hardware, hinges. Every surface is then sanded, cleaned, and repaired. Dents, scratches, and imperfections are filled and smoothed for a flawless base.

03

Priming

A high-adhesion primer is applied to ensure the topcoat bonds properly and lasts. We select the right primer for each material — wood, laminate, MDF, or metal — to guarantee durability.

04

Spray Application

Using our professional spray booth, we apply multiple thin, even coats of your chosen colour and finish. Spray application delivers the smoothest, most consistent results — a true factory-quality look.

05

Reassembly & Quality Check

Once fully cured, we reassemble the piece, reinstall hardware, and conduct a thorough quality inspection. Every surface is checked before we notify you that your piece is ready for pickup.
